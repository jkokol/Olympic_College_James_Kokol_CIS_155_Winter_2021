What is Magic the Gathering?

Magic the Gathering is a card game developed by Wizards of the Coast in 1993. Since its inception, the game has become one of the most popular card games of all time. As such, the best stratagies, or meta, of the game has changed significatnly over time. Many players from all walks of life, from the average collector, to the world champions, use these stratagies in order to seek victory. However, some may forget about the metas of the past. That is where this page comes in.

What is this webpage about?

This website sees to find all forms of Magic decks from many metas, and explain what made them so successful, how the game as a whole reacted to that deck, and if it is possible to play the deck today. Addtionally, This page will act as a history lession throughout magic's unique history. Join me, as we look through the powerful decks from accross the Magic landscape.
